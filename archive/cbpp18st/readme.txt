Contacts Between Police and the Public 2018 - Statistical Tables 		
		
This zip archive contains tables in individual  .csv spreadsheets		
from Contacts Between Police and the Public 2018 - Statistical Tables,  NCJ 255730.  The full report including text		
and graphics in pdf format is available from: https://www.bjs.gov/index.cfm?ty=pbdetail&iid=7167		
		
This report is one in a series.  More recent editions may be available.		
To view a list of all in the series go to https://www.bjs.gov/index.cfm?ty=pbse&sid=18		
		
Filename		Table title
cbpp18stt01.csv		Table 1. Number and percent of U.S. residents age 16 or older with any police contact, by type of contact and demographic characteristics, 2018
cbpp18stt02.csv		Table 2. Residents with police contact, by reason for contact, 2015 and 2018
cbpp18stt03.csv		Table 3. Residents who experienced nonfatal threats or use of force during contacts with police, by demographic characteristics, 2015 and 2018
cbpp18stt04.csv		Table 4. Residents who experienced nonfatal threats or use of force during their most recent police-initiated contact or traffic-accident contact, by resident perception of force as necessary or excessive and demographic characteristics, 2015 and 2018
cbpp18stt05.csv		Table 5. Percent of residents who experienced nonfatal threats or use of force during their most recent police-initiated contact or traffic-accident contact, by resident race or ethnicity and type of force, 2018
